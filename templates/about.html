03 : About me

I like self-learning

I see knowledge as a way to connect with the world. Also, I believe that you don't need school in order to learn things, it's okay to learn things without getting a diploma. So, I take self-learning as key to my life.

My goal

My goal is to become a roboticist someday. I believe robotics can help a lot of people to achieve a better world, one we can give to future generations.

My dreams

I want to do a lot of stuff. I want to understand mathematics and physics better to understand better the world around me. Also, I'd like to find a way to make knowledge affordable accesible to anyone, as much as it is to me. In general, I just want to share the luck I consider I have.
